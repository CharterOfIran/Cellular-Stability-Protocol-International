To ensure absolute security, if you intend to participate in the management or technical aspects of this project, you must not use your primary/personal account.

### üõ† Steps for a Secure Account:
1. **Secure Access:** Perform all account creation and management steps exclusively through browsers based on an anonymous network (Tor) or IP-masking tools with no DNS leaks.
2. **Anonymous Email Service:** Use email services that:
   * Do not require a phone number or personal recovery email for registration.
   * Use end-to-end encryption protocols.
   * Avoid famous commercial services that share data with regulatory authorities.
3. **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** GitHub may require a number for verification. Instead of real/local numbers, use:
   * Authenticator Apps that work offline to generate one-time codes.
   * Virtual numbers with no link to your physical identity or SIM card.
4. **Profile Settings:**
   * Do not use your real name, personal photo, or a biography that reveals your personal catchphrases.
5. **Preventing Leaks in Commits:** After creating the account, ensure your system's Git settings are configured to hide your email in commit history.
