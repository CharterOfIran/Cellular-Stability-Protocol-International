This document is designed to prevent the looting of national wealth, the destruction of banks, and the loss of citizens' property records during the first 72 hours of transition.

## 1. Protection of Banks and Treasuries
* **Physical Closure:** All bank branches must be closed immediately, and their entrances sealed by local volunteers and trusted staff.
* **Freeze on Liquidity Outflow:** The withdrawal of large cash sums, gold, or jewelry from bank vaults is strictly prohibited until the establishment of the interim government.
* **ATM Protection:** Volunteers must prevent the destruction of ATMs; these machines are essential for the limited distribution of liquidity in the second phase.

## 2. Safeguarding Records (Registry Offices and Judiciary)
* **Sealing Archives:** Archive rooms containing land titles, housing deeds, and citizens' judicial records must be physically sealed in the presence of guild representatives and the Neighborhood Council.
* **Data Servers:** IT technicians are tasked with cutting remote access to national databases and physically isolating servers to prevent the intentional deletion of records.

## 3. Protection of Museums and Cultural Centers
* These centers are our historical wealth. The Neighborhood Council is responsible for deploying teams of trusted volunteers for **unarmed guarding** around museums to prevent the smuggling of antiquities.

## 4. Prohibition of Forced Occupation
* Any occupation of government buildings or private properties during these 72 hours is illegal and will be prosecuted as a civil crime in transition-period courts.
