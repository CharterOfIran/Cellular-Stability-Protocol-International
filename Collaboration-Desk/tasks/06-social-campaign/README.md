### 1. Mission
This seat is dedicated to protecting the **lives of innocent and defenseless individuals** whose only shield against danger is awareness. Our objective is to organize a collective movement to deliver the Charter's content to the farthest corners of society. Every share is not merely a virtual activity, but a tangible effort to awaken the collective conscience and save the lives of those whose voices are not heard.

### 2. National Public Participation (Roadmap)
This is not a technical call; it is a manifestation of national solidarity. Every citizen, regardless of their social media reach, can be a part of this "Collective Echo":
* **The Power of Unified Hashtags:** Synchronized use of consensus hashtags (e.g., #DestinySharing) to break through censorship barriers and amplify the voices of the marginalized globally.
* **Every Citizen a Medium:** Redistributing content within family, local, and professional groups (e.g., teachers, physicians, workers) to provide direct awareness at the heart of the community.
* **Message Simplification:** Transforming Charter concepts into short, impactful social media stories that emphasize the preservation of human life and dignity.
